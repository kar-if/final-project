Black Women in Coding

In the dynamic world of technology, the representation and achievements of black women in coding have been historically overlooked. However, the landscape is changing as a new wave of talented and resilient black women are making their mark in the field.Black women have made significant contributions to the field of coding and computer science throughout history, despite facing numerous challenges and systemic barriers.
